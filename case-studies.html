EdTech SaaS — Scaling User Acquisition via Paid Social

The Challenge

An EdTech SaaS company needed to scale its mobile app install campaigns while reducing cost-per-acquisition. They were over-reliant on a single channel (Meta) with declining efficiency and no structured testing framework for creatives or audiences.

Strategy

  • Diversified paid social spend across Meta, TikTok, and Google App Campaigns
  • Built structured A/B testing framework across creative formats and audience segments
  • Implemented Appsflyer attribution for accurate cross-channel measurement
  • Created lookalike audiences based on high-LTV user segments

YouTube Ads PMax App Growth

YouTube & PMax — Campaign Optimization

The Challenge

YouTube and Performance Max campaigns were underperforming — high CPVs, low view-through rates, and poor conversion from video engagement to app installs. The campaign structure lacked segmentation and creative variation.

Strategy

  • Restructured YouTube campaigns with segmented ad groups by audience intent
  • Created short-form video variants (6s, 15s, 30s) optimized for mobile viewing
  • Implemented PMax asset group testing with creative signal optimization
  • Built custom audience segments based on YouTube engagement data

Attribution Analytics Data Strategy

Multi-Touch Attribution Framework

The Challenge

With campaigns running across 4+ platforms, attribution was fragmented. Last-click attribution was overvaluing bottom-funnel channels while undervalueing awareness-stage campaigns (YouTube, TikTok). Budget allocation decisions were based on incomplete data.

Strategy

  • Implemented Appsflyer as the single source of truth for attribution
  • Designed multi-touch attribution model weighting both view-through and click-through conversions
  • Integrated CRM data (CleverTap) for post-install event tracking and LTV analysis
  • Built automated Power BI dashboards for real-time budget reallocation

Results

0%

Attribution Accuracy

0%

Budget Efficiency

0

Channels Unified

0

Single Source of Truth

Tools Used

Appsflyer CleverTap Power BI Meta CAPI Google Analytics

Key Learnings

Multi-touch attribution revealed that YouTube campaigns were driving 30% more assisted conversions than last-click data showed. Integrating CRM data with attribution enabled LTV-based optimization, shifting budget toward channels acquiring users with higher lifetime value.

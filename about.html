My Story

Marketing, for me, isn't just about running ads — it's about solving business problems with creativity and data. I'm Farhan Bagwan, a growth-focused marketing strategist who thrives at the intersection of analytical thinking and digital innovation.

With an MSc in Management (Distinction) from a UK Russell Group university and hands-on experience in B2C SaaS user acquisition, I've developed a unique perspective. I approach marketing like a consultant — starting with business objectives, mapping the funnel, and then engineering campaigns that deliver measurable impact.

My expertise spans the full paid social ecosystem — from Meta and TikTok ad campaigns to mobile attribution with Appsflyer. I'm equally comfortable building dashboards in Power BI as I am crafting audience segmentation strategies. This blend of strategic thinking and technical execution is what sets me apart.
